About Us

Optix Pakistan Private Limited is a new age information and communication company providing Quad play services (Broadband, Telephony, CATV, Surveillance) to household customers. Since our establishment in the year 2015 we have served connectivity and entertainment needs of households in Pakistan efficiently setting new benchmarks of customer services. Our network topology is based on GPON which leads to low OPEX and ability to withstand the future high-speed data needs).

With Optify we aim to provide Wi-Fi services to our consumers on the go. So, that everyone can enjoy connectivity regardless of locational constraints.
